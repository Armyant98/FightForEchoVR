Save Echo Vr

Since its inception, Echo VR has been one of the most successful games on the meta store along with one of the largest organically grown communities of passionate players. Generating competitive leagues, huge organized LAN events, and even a professionally paid league, Echo VR can only be labeled an absolute success. Now it is being permanently shut down forever along with everything created along the way.
